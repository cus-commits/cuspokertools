About cusPokerTools

cusPokerTools is a client-side poker equity calculator. All computations run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to any server.

Features:

Hold'em and Omaha equity with exhaustive enumeration or Monte Carlo simulation. The Double Board PLO Bomb Pot mode calculates equity across two independent boards with scoop percentages.

Card notation: Rank (2-9, T, J, Q, K, A) + suit (h, d, c, s). Example: AhKs = Ace of hearts, King of spades.

AI Assistant: Describe poker scenarios in plain English. The AI will parse your scenario, ask clarifying questions, run equity calculations, and provide analysis. Requires a Claude API key (set in AI Assistant settings).

AI Assistant Settings
